Our Story

A Village Soul
in Every Bite

Gauwala — meaning "of the village" — was born from a deep love of Bengal's rustic culinary traditions. Nestled in West Bengal, we bring the warmth of a village hearth to every plate we serve.

From slow-cooked curries perfected over generations to freshly brewed masala chai, every dish is a journey through the heartlands of India. We believe food is not just nourishment — it is memory, it is culture, it is home.
